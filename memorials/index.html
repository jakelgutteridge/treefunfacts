Memorials

Many people choose to remember a loved one by placing a memorial plaque on a bench or tree in Cedars Park. If you would like to purchase a memorial, please visit the Broxbourne Borough Council web page.

The Council do not publish their memorial data for the park, so volunteers from the Friends of Cedars Park have compiled information about the personal memorials in the park and you can now refer to the Cedars Park Memorial Listing.

Great War Centennial Corner

The Great War Centennial Corner in Cedars Park remembers all those who were lost in the First World War.
