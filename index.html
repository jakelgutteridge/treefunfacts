Tree Fun Facts

This website is dedicated to all 3 trillion trees around the world and how they are amazing creations of nature. Trees are extremely important to our planet and we hope that they can become better appreciated members of our ecosystem.

In order to truly understand the value and wonder of trees, we need to look at them in different ways and understand the roles that they play, such as from mythological, historical, religious and scientific perspectives. They are not only the oldest living organisms in the world, but are an indispensable life source – as such, trees should be highly regarded and respected. We hope that you will find this website useful and enjoy expanding your knowledge about trees!
