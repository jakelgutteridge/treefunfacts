Birds

Many different bird species and varieties have been observed within Cedars Park. These include the native British species that are typically found in parks, but we are also aware of several exotic types, including Indian and Australian parakeets, that reside in our park.

If you have seen a bird not mentioned on this list, we would love to know at webmaster@cedarspark.info.
